Grocery Store Produce

Fruits

  • Apples: A round fruit with red, green, or yellow skin and a white, crispy interior.
  • Bananas: A long, curved fruit with yellow skin and a soft, creamy interior.
  • Oranges: A round citrus fruit with a thick orange skin and a juicy, tangy interior.
  • Strawberries: A small, red fruit with seeds on the outside and a sweet, juicy interior.
  • Grapes: Small, round fruits that grow in clusters and can be green, red, or purple in color.

Vegetables

  • Carrots: A long, thin orange vegetable with a sweet, crispy interior.
  • Cucumbers: A green, cylindrical vegetable with a cool, refreshing interior.
  • Lettuce: A leafy green vegetable used in salads and sandwiches.
  • Peas: Small, round green vegetables that grow in pods and have a sweet, delicate flavor.
  • Tomatoes: A round, red vegetable with a juicy, tangy interior and a soft skin.

Herbs

  • Basil: A fragrant, green herb with a sweet, spicy flavor commonly used in Italian cooking.
  • Cilantro: A green herb with a pungent, citrusy flavor commonly used in Mexican and Asian cuisine.
  • Mint: A refreshing, green herb with a cool, invigorating flavor commonly used in drinks and desserts.
  • Rosemary: A woody, fragrant herb with a sharp, pine-like flavor commonly used in Mediterranean cooking.
  • Thyme: A delicate, fragrant herb with a warm, slightly bitter flavor commonly used in French cuisine.
